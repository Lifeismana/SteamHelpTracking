[h1]Setting Client Rates (Limit Download Speed) in Steam[/h1]

What are the best client rates for my internet connection?

[section style=note]

[h3]Note:[/h3]
Client rate settings are useful in troubleshooting lag and poor multiplayer performance. Please see the [url=https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=5139-YADK-4769]Troubleshooting Lag[/url] topic for further recommendations.
[/section]

[h4]Steam Download Speed[/h4]
If you notice connection problems during Steam downloads, or want to limit how much bandwidth Steam can use, you can find these settings by doing the following.

[olist]

[*]Go to the upper left in the Steam client and select [b]Steam[/b] > [b]Settings[/b] from the drop down menu

[*]Select the Downloads tab from the window that appears
[img]{STEAM_CLAN_IMAGE}/39983882/1e76c99f1a9949d0604d4fd2af9529b65caea6b4.png[/img]

[*]Here you will find different download settings for Steam including the [b]Limit Download Speed[/b] setting. If you enable it you can set the limit you want.

[b]Note:[/b] The rates listed here are in kilo[b]bytes[/b] and mega[b]bytes[/b] a second, while most Internet connections are rated in [b]bits[/b] per second.

[/olist]
