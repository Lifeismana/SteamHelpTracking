[section id=wherecontainer]

[h2]I scanned a container and it is no longer in my Inventory. Where did it go?[/h2]
Scanned containers are consumed, meaning they will no longer appear in your Counter-Strike 2 or Steam Inventories. Scanned containers cannot be traded or sold on the Steam Community Market.
[/section]

[section id=seecontents]

[h2]Can I scan a container, see the contents, then trade or sell the container on the Community Market?[/h2]
No. A scanned container cannot be traded or sold on the Steam Community Market.
[/section]

[section id=scananother]

[h2]I have scanned a container and the item has been revealed. How do I scan another container?[/h2]
Once a container has been scanned and the item has been revealed, the only way to scan another container is to purchase and claim the previously revealed item.
[/section]

[section id=resetcontainer]

[h2]Can I reset a container’s contents or scan the same container more than once?[/h2]
No. Containers cannot be scanned more than once. Once scanned, the item inside the container is locked and cannot be changed.
[/section]

[section id=diffcontainers]

[h2]Can I scan multiple containers if they are different types?[/h2]
No. Only one container of any kind (Weapon Case, Sticker Capsule, Souvenir Package, etc.) can be scanned at a time.
[/section]

[section id=scansouv]

[h2]How do I open keyless containers?[/h2]
Keyless containers like Souvenir Packages can be opened on their own and do not require the X-Ray Scanner.
[/section]
