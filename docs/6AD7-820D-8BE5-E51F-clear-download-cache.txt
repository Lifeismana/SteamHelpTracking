[h1]Clear download cache[/h1]

Clearing your download cache can solve problems with games that won't download or start.

[section style=note]

[h3]Note:[/h3]
This process will not affect your currently installed games, but you will need to log into Steam afterwards.
[/section]

[olist]

[*]From your Steam Client, open the Settings panel by selecting "Steam > Settings" from the top left client menu.

[*]In the Settings panel, select the Downloads tab and you'll find the "Clear Download Cache" button at the bottom.

[exclude_realm=china][img]{STEAM_CLAN_IMAGE}/39983882/8ca2b502a0c0c42f498be1395129ab6d84f54fcf.png[/img]
[/exclude_realm]

[exclude_realm=global][img]http://steamcdn-a.akamaihd.net/steam/support/faq/chts/clear_download_cache.jpg[/img]
[/exclude_realm]

[*]Then select "Confirm" to acknowledge this will clear your download cache and that you'll need to login to Steam again.

[exclude_realm=china][img]{STEAM_CLAN_IMAGE}/39983882/97c1713ec0e2d395f56f344094f85ba6341cb714.png[/img]
[/exclude_realm]

[exclude_realm=global][img]http://steamcdn-a.akamaihd.net/steam/support/faq/chts/confirm_clear_cache.jpg[/img]
[/exclude_realm]

[/olist]

If you're still having trouble with your downloads and any games that won't start, please contact [url=https://help.steampowered.com/]Steam Support[/url].
