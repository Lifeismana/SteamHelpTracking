[h4]I already bought Alyx![/h4]
You will receive Half-Life: Alyx when you complete your Valve Index payment. If you previously purchased the game on Steam, the purchase price will be credited around the time your hardware ships.

[h4]Will I receive bonus content for Half-Life: Alyx?[/h4]
Alyx bonus content was available for Index orders placed before March 25, 2020.

More info is available on the official [url=https://www.half-life.com/en/alyx/]Alyx website[/url].

[h4]Questions about your Valve Index order?[/h4]
Check out our [url=https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=1072-AXCV-5863]Index Order FAQ[/url].

[h4]How to turn off preorder skins[/h4]
To disable the Valve Index Preorder weapon skins, right-click Half-Life: Alyx in your Steam library, and choose Properties... > DLC. Uncheck the "install" checkbox next to “Half-Life: Alyx – Index Preorder Items”. To enable them, re-check the checkbox.
