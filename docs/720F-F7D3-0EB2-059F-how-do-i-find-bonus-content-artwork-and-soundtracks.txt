[h1]How do I find bonus content, artwork and soundtracks?[/h1]

How do I find the following content that comes with my purchase?

[list]

[*]Digital Artwork

[*]Soundtracks

[*]Digital Deluxe Content

[*]Other/Misc Content
[/list]

All [url=https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=2668-CFOM-1881]Downloadable Content (DLC)[/url] access should automatically be added to the product. Please contact the product's support for assistance with DLC content.

[h1]In most cases, bonus content can be found within the products's downloaded files:[/h1]
To access this content please do the following...

[olist]

[*]Right click the product in your Steam Library and select "Properties".
[*]Under the "Local Files" tab, select "Browse...".

[*]Within the product's local files, locate a folder that has a similar name to the content you are searching for.

[/olist]

Please note, the folder containing the bonus material may have a different title, but should be identifiable as the location for this content. All Soundtracks should be in MP3 format, while Art Books should be a PDF or ePub.

[h1]Where can I find Soundtracks within Steam?[/h1]
Some soundtracks are automatically integrated into your Steam Library. Please visit our [url=https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=5460-QTPM-9243]Soundtracks in Steam[/url] FAQ for more info. Soundtracks that are not automatically added can be found using the instructions above.
