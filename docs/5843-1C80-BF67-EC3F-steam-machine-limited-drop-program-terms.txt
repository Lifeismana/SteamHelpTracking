You must be a legal resident of the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, any EU Member State, or Australia, at least 18 years old, and the registered account holder of an unlimited Steam account who has made one purchase prior to April 27, 2026. No purchase is required to register for the chance to buy the Product. If you are selected for an opportunity to buy the Product, the purchase is optional. Limit one Product purchase per household. We will use information such as shipping address and/or payment information to eliminate multiple entries.

[h3]Selection:[/h3]

After the Registration Period closes, Valve will randomly select the eligible registrants from among all eligible registrations received for an opportunity to buy the Product. Odds of being selected depend on the number of eligible registrations received.

Prices are subject to change. Those emails will include a link for purchasing the Product (“Link”). The Link will expire 72 hours after it is sent by Valve. If you do not buy the Product by the expiration date, your opportunity will also expire. If you are not selected, you will receive an email letting you know that you’ve been added to the waitlist and we'll let you know when more units become available.
