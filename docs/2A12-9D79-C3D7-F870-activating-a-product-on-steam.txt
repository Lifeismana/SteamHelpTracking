[h1]Activating a Product on Steam[/h1]

How do I register a CD Key to activate a product on Steam?

Please follow these instructions to activate a new retail purchase on Steam:

[olist]

[*]Launch the Steam client software and log into your Steam account.

[*]Click the [b]Games[/b] menu option at the top of the Steam client.

[*]Choose [b]Activate a Product on Steam...[/b]

[*]Follow the onscreen instructions to complete the process.

[/olist]

Please see the [url=https://support.steampowered.com/kb_article.php?ref=7480-WUSF-3601]Retail CD Keys[/url] article for more info on registering keys.

[h4]Register through browser[/h4]

You can also register a CD key through a browser [url=https://store.steampowered.com/account/registerkey]here.[/url]
