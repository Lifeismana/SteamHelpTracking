[h1]CS2 - In-game Items[/h1]

[section style=warning]Any Counter-Strike 2 item received in trade or purchased from the Community Market cannot be transacted again for seven days.

Detailed information on this restriction can be found on the [url=https://blog.counter-strike.net/index.php/2018/03/20308/]Counter-Strike blog[/url].
[/section]
